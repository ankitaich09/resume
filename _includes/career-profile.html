Career Profile

Hello, welcome to my website. I am Ankit Aich. Currently, I am pursuing my doctorate in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Chicago.
My primary area of research is Natural Language Processing. My specific interests lie in the area of figurative speech detection. Primarily, I aim to detect subtle nuances in speech, and figurative language such as humor using sarcasm or metaphor. My resarch has led me to use a variety of both classical and multimodal NLP techniques to achieve my research goals.

I currently work at the Natural Language Processing Lab at UIC and work under Dr. Natalie Parde, who is my advisor and the co-director of the NLP lab at the University of Illinois at Chicago.

I also have a variety of other interests, which range from creating and playing music, to teaching, reading books, and practicing martial arts. All of which I try and pursue as I make my way through a very interesting journey called life.
